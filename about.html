Vision Section

Primary Goal

Our Vision

We aim to create a lasting tribute that honours the sacrifices of Indian and Canadian soldiers while inspiring future generations with the values of humanity and freedom. Through the Brothers in Arms Memorial, we aim to foster awareness, remembrance, and unity for a more compassionate world.

News and Events Section

Main Purpose

Our Mission

Awareness

We strive to educate the public about the remarkable collaboration between our soldiers, ensuring they are recognized and celebrated.

Remembrance

Our memorial stands as a tribute to the brave soldiers whose sacrifices shaped history. By honouring their legacy and valour.

Inspiration

We aim to ignite hope in future generations. We share enduring values of unity and freedom that encourage ongoing acts of bravery.

Features Section

Features

A Living Legacy

The park features scenic pathways, stone installations, a pond, landscaped trails with plaques and QR codes, and 108 trees planted as a "Living Guard of Honour." At its heart, a hand-carved 8×6 granite memorial with detailed battlefield scenes honouring the soldiers' courage and sacrifice.

Phases Section

The Process

Building the Memorial

(Completed)

Phase One

The initial development of the memorial site included the planting of 108 trees and the preparation of the groundwork for the location.

(On-Going)

Phase Two

Community support will fund the creation and installation of a granite memorial and other memorial elements, honouring their legacy.

(Coming Soon)

Phase Three

Scenic pathways, stone installations, a pond, and a hand-carved granite memorial will be developed to complete the site.

Srishti Section

Our Organization

Srishti Foundation

Founded in 2012, Srishti (सृष्टि) Foundation is a Canadian non-profit inspiring selfless service, celebrating Indian traditions, and empowering youth. As the force behind the Brothers in Arms Memorial, Srishti unites communities with timeless compassion.
