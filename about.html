Tribute to Bravery

A tribute park in London, Ontario, where scenic routes, interactive exhibits, a symbolic arrangement of trees, and a hand-carved granite memorial bring the stories of shared courage and sacrifice to life.

Vision Section

Vision

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a lasting tribute that honours the sacrifices of Indian and Canadian soldiers while inspiring future generations with the values of humanity and freedom. Through the Brothers in Arms Memorial, we aim to foster awareness, remembrance, and unity for a more compassionate world.

Features Section

Features

A Living Legacy

The park features scenic pathways, stone installations, a pond, landscaped trails with plaques and QR codes, and 108 trees planted as a "Living Guard of Honour." At its heart, a hand-carved 8×6 granite memorial with detailed battlefield scenes honouring the soldiers' courage and sacrifice.

Srishti Section

Foundation

Srishti Foundation

Founded in 2012, Srishti (सृष्टि) Foundation is a Canadian non-profit inspiring selfless service, celebrating Indian traditions, and empowering youth. As the force behind the Brothers in Arms Memorial, Srishti unites communities with timeless compassion.
