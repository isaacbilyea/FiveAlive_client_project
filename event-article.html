Holi 2025: London's Biggest Festival of Colours Celebration Yet

Date: April 27, 2025 | Time: 12:00 PM to 5:00 PM | Location: Victoria Park, London, ON

For the 12th consecutive year since 2012, Srishti Foundation transformed downtown London into a vibrant celebration of Holi, drawing approximately 4,000 people to Victoria Park. This ancient Dharmic festival brought together people from all backgrounds in a joyous riot of colours, music, and dance.

A Day of Unity and Celebration

The celebration kicked off at noon with a traditional Ganesh Puja performed by Pandit Tiwari Ji, setting the tone for a day of unity in diversity, love, and happiness. The event was honored by the presence of London Mayor Josh Morgan, along with city councillors and members of Parliament and Provincial Legislature.

Thousands of attendees participate in synchronized colour throws throughout the day.

Entertainment and Activities

The festival featured:

  • High-energy folk dance performances
  • Live drummers and DJ music
  • Synchronized colour throws
  • Traditional food vendors
  • Community youth performances

Cultural Significance

The event concluded with a traditional Aarti at 5:00 PM, with participants joining in devotional dance. Throughout the day, attendees enjoyed a variety of Indian and multicultural cuisines from on-site food vendors.
