The Brothers in Arms Memorial

Over a century ago, Indian and Canadian soldiers stood shoulder to shoulder on the battlefields of World War I, united by courage and sacrifice. These brave souls forged an unbreakable bond across borders, cultures, and continents. Yet, their stories remain untold in the pages of history.

Through the 'Brothers in Arms' memorial, we aim to illuminate their shared sacrifice and celebrate the unity that shaped a better world. But we need your voice to keep their legacy alive.
