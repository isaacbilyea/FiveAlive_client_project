Brothers in Arms

The Brothers in Arms Memorial stands as North America's first tribute honouring the alliance between Indian and Canadian soldiers during World War I. Your support is vital in bringing the Brothers in Arms Memorial to life and preserving it for future generations.

History Section

History

A Shared Legacy

In World War I, over 135,000 Indian soldiers fought alongside Canadian forces in pivotal battles like Ypres, the Somme, and Vimy. Their timely arrival in 1914 helped prevent a German breakthrough, shaping the course of the war.
