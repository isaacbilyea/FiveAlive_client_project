Memorial Design Unveiled
News

The final design for the Brothers in Arms Memorial has been revealed, featuring symbolic elements representing both Indian and Canadian military heritage. The design includes traditional Indian architectural elements alongside Canadian maple leaves.

History Section

History

A Shared Legacy

In World War I, over 135,000 Indian soldiers fought alongside Canadian forces in pivotal battles like Ypres, the Somme, and Vimy. Their timely arrival in 1914 helped prevent a German breakthrough, shaping the course of the war.
