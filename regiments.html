Regiments of
World War One

Here are the Indian and Canadian regiments that served alongside each other, highlighting their unique histories, identities, and contributions. Despite coming from different parts of the world, these regiments shared a commitment to duty, discipline, and camaraderie, leaving a lasting legacy in military history.
