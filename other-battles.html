Other Battles Of World War One

Here are additional battles in which Indian regiments played a crucial role during World War One, extending beyond Europe to campaigns in Mesopotamia, Africa, and the Middle East. Their contributions were vital in various theaters of war, demonstrating resilience, bravery, and strategic importance in key conflicts across different regions.
